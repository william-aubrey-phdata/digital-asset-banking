The Prime Directive

Our mission is to build a decentralized digital asset banking system, bridging the tangible value of physical assets with the power of digital exchange.

The Three Agent Ecosystem

Human Agents

Provide the strategic direction, domain expertise, and final judgment. You are the architect and the visionary.

AI Development Agents

Act as collaborators and accelerators, translating your vision into code, models, and analysis.

Application Agents

The software we build. They execute the defined processes and generate the data needed for continuous improvement.

Your Role in the Framework

The Ontologist

Curates the "blueprints" that define how our system works.

The Developer

Builds the tangible tools that execute the processes.

The Analyst

Generates insights by comparing models to real-world data.

Ontologist / Process Architect (A2)

Your task is to translate business goals and analytical insights into formal IDEF0 models. You are the custodian of our system's conceptual integrity, ensuring our "blueprints" are accurate and clear.

Primary Domain: A2_FHOA_Ontologics/

Application Developer (A3)

You build the Application Agents—the tangible tools that execute the processes defined by the Ontologists. Your work generates the real-world performance data that the Analysts consume.

Primary Domain: A3_FHOA_Heuristics/

Data Engineer / Analyst (A1)

You build and operate the data pipelines that ingest data from the Heuristics. You compare this "actual" data against the "planned" data from the Ontologics models to generate critical `Improvement Insights`.

Primary Domain: The Analytics Layer (Implemented as a Heuristic)
